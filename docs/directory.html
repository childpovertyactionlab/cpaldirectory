Project Directory

All projects listed in this directory contain a set of four icons describing what CPAL pillar, update cadence, intended audience, and product type the project is. Additionally, projects are classified into one of three sections.

  • Active: A product that is currently utilized by its intended audience, maintaining a live status with ongoing support. It undergoes regular updates at defined cadences to ensure its functionality, security, and relevance remain optimal for user needs.

  • In Progress: A product or feature currently under development, which is expected to be launched or significantly updated within the next 6 months. This phase involves active design, development, and testing to prepare the product for its release to the intended audience.

  • Upcoming Revamp: A product that is operational and in use but is scheduled for significant updates or a makeover to improve functionality, user experience, or to incorporate new features. The revamp is typically planned and announced in advance to prepare the user base for changes.

  • Model Update: A product, especially one reliant on data models or algorithms, scheduled for a systematic update. These updates are often to improve accuracy, efficiency, or adapt to new data. It usually doesnâ€™t overhaul the entire product but ensures that the core components are modernized and in line with current standards or needs.

  • Deprecated: A product that has become outdated or is no longer supported with updates. It was built on older technology or methodologies and may no longer be used actively or recommended for use. Plans for its future might include phasing it out completely or considering it for a major overhaul.

2024

CPAL Internal Metrics Dashboard (2024) Upcoming Revamp

This shiny dashboard is an internal reference tool which various pillars may use in order to find metrics across various topic areas across a number of different geographies (census tracts, zip codes, counties, and cities) from 2017 to present.

[Community Resource Explorer (2024)] Model Update

The Community Resource Explorer is an external mapping tool receiving an update to enhance its capability in showcasing neighborhood assets and needs. Itâ€™s designed to guide impactful decisions and initiatives by individuals and institutions alike.

Children Affected by Poverty (2024)

This mapping tool is being developed to quantify and visualize the number of children affected by poverty across the City of Dallas. Its aim is to facilitate understanding and action planning for organizational responsibilities and interventions.

[Place and Equity in the City of Dallas (2024)] In Progress

This internal web report provides a comprehensive analysis of equity across various sectors in Dallas, contrasted with other cities. It leverages a systems change approach, focusing on key areas like human development, economic vitality, and governance.

Court Observers (2024) In Progress

Court Observers is an external fact sheet serving as a link repository for students participating in the Dallas County Court Observation project. It provides students with essential information about their roles and responsibilities, along with relevant project details.

[Benefits Delivery (SNAP) in Dallas County (2024)] In Progress

This internal web report offers a yearly analysis of SNAP benefit access across Dallas County. It provides comparative insights with other cities and includes a high-fidelity tool for detailed geographic assessments within Dallas.

[Trust Her Clinic Reimbursement (2024)] Upcoming Revamp

An impending update to the Trust Her Clinic Reimbursement system is on the horizon. Shifting to a Shiny based dashboard, it will enhance the user experience for clinics affiliated with Trust Her, moving away from the current Tableau system.

WIC Uptake Tool (2024) In Progress

The WIC Uptake Tool is a dynamic dashboard designed for power users, offering monthly updates on WIC participation across Dallas County. It provides a deep dive into Local Agency 7 clinics, enabling targeted insights and actions.

2023

Justice40 in Dallas County (2023)

A mapping report visualizing a number of Justice40 initiative metrics for Dallas County.

Neighborhood Safety Dashboard (2023)

A prototype dashboard centered around bcWorkshop neighborhoods in the City of Dallas where users can review general metrics about when specified incidents occur within a neighborhood and what kinds of incidents occur.

CPAL Talking Points Report (2023)

A web report collating a number of city wide metrics important to organizational goals across all Child Poverty Action Lab domain areas. Currently focused on metrics related to Child Poverty.

Community Displacement Tool (2023)

Analysis conducted with the aid of the Charles Schwab Community Development and Investment Team in order to assess where the areas of highest risk for displacement of residents in Dallas County may be when viewed through the lens of property level changes such as evictions, property value changes, ownership changes, and new building permits across time.

Birth Rate Explorer (2023) In Progress

Birth Rate and Clinic Explorer tool developed by Hyperobjekt for CPAL.

RAPID Survey Report (2023)

A web report that is intended to be updated quarterly in order to visualize new survey responses from households participating in the RAPID survey. The households participating in the survey are the same throughout the course of the project, responses are intended to show how households views on topics change over time throughout the City of Dallas.

WIC Clinic Reports (2023)

An internal-external tool intended to aid WIC Clinics and WIC Clinic Managers in assessing needs and performance based on key metrics identified by Early Development Domain leads at the Child Poverty Action Lab.

Dallas County Court Observation Report (2023)

A web report visualizing eviction court case proceedings observed across Dallas County through efforts led by the Child Poverty Action Lab and aided by the SMU School of Law.

Dallas ISD Campus Housing Profiles (2023)

This is a tool generated to give Dallas ISD information related to middle of the year moves and evictions in attendance zones for all school campuses.

[Trust Her Clinic Reimbursement (2023)]

A private tableau dashboard intended to help produce reimbursement estimates for Trust Her partner clinics.

2022

North Texas Evictions Project (2022)

North Texas Evictions was developed with the intention of aiding advocacy partners in North Texas with actionable insight.

Daily Eviction Filings (2022)

Spreadsheet shared with partner organizations containing new daily eviction case filings in Dallas County.

North Texas Small-Area Fair Market Rent (2022)

Comparison of current Small-Area Fair Market rent from HUD against the average rents for apartment complexes in North Texas across various bedroom sizes.

Publicly Owned Land in Dallas County (2022)

Review of all publicly owned land in the City of Dallas by owner type. Intended use is to understand how much land owned by the City of Dallas is available for development.

Afterschool Landscape Tool (2022)

An analysis generated for Dallas Afterschool intended to aid them in understanding the current landscape of after school programming need relative to programs they currently offer.

CUSP Housing Quality Analysis (2022)

An analysis generated by the NYU CUSP Team as a two semester program. The project was intended to review the state of housing quality in the City of Dallas and estimate need for housing repairs across City of Dallas Census Tracts.

Urban Blight Explorer (2022)

Machine learning model of select neighborhoods in the City of Dallas intended to spot areas of blight across the city based on photos captured by a mobile photo car.

Child Care Deserts (2022)

An analysis of areas of high need for child care centers across North Texas based on capacity of child care centers by type.

Teen Birth Rates (2022) Deprecated

A dashboard visualizing teen birth rates across North Texas using data from the North Texas Hospital Council Foundation. To be superseded by the Birth Rate Explorer.

North Texas Social Mobility Hub (2021)

The Social Mobility Hub was developed in conjunction with UNTD in understand the barriers and drivers towards upward mobility, and how the chances for upward mobility can be increased for all communities.

Early Development Overview Rates (2021) Deprecated

A mapping tool visualizing a number of American Community Survey Metrics related to the Early Development space.

City of Dallas Lighting Analysis (2022)

An analysis reviewing areas of highest need and ROI for LED lighting improvements across the City of Dallas.

WIC Participation Report (2020) Deprecated
